Maps

Below is a collection of Forest Service Maps to navigate Kentucky's Red River Gorge area.
They include information about current trails and campsites.
As always with trails at the Red, make sure to bring plenty of water, food, and to dress properly for the trail you're doing!
Make sure to leave no trace as well. This includes not geotagging photos, and packing out what you pack in.

This page is a work in progress! I plan to add more of my own information about each trail in the future. So be on the lookout for that on this page. Happy trails!
