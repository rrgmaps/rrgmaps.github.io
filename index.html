Maps

Below is a collection of personal maps that I've made to navigate Kentucky's Red River Gorge area.
They include information about current official and unofficial trails.
As always with trails at the Red, make sure to bring plenty of water, food, and to dress properly for the trail you're doing!
Always make sure to leave no trace as well. This includes not geotagging photos, and packing out what you pack in.

Maps are based on Hinterlands by Jerrell Goodpaster, with inspiration from my own trips to the gorge.
More guides can be found at the Red River Underground YouTube channel. Definitely go give these guys some love for amazing trail guides!
